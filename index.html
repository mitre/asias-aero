Enhancing Aviation Safety through Collaboration

A collaborative government-industry partnership on aviation safety data sharing & analysis

  • Mission > Analyze shared data to identify system-wide hazards before accidents or incidents occur
  • Goal > Gain real-world insights on system-wide aviation hazards through analysis of shared data, leading to timely hazard mitigation and prevention of incidents and accidents
  • Differentiator > Enabling a system-wide approach to aviation safety through collaboration and data sharing
